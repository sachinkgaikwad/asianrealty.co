Property Description

Introducing Riva: where modernity meets affordability in the heart of NIBM. Our 1 & 2 BHK Spacious Apartments redefine urban living with their contemporary design and emphasis on safety. Set within a single tower on a lush one-acre landscape, RIVA offers 11 storeys of elegant living. Residents can unwind in the dedicated children's play area or socialize in the club house and landscaped garden. With schools within a 3-kilometer radius and major hospitals nearby, convenience is assured.

Parking is hassle-free with basement and ground floor options, while 24/7 water supply and generator backup ensure comfort. Each apartment boasts LPG gas piping, CCTV monitoring, and Wi-Fi access for added convenience. Experience luxury with LED lights, false ceilings, and premium flooring throughout. Step onto spacious balconies for stunning views of the surroundings.

RIVA combines the vibrancy of city life with the tranquility of a sanctuary, making it the ideal home. Don't miss the chance to live in this oasis of style and peace. Visit RIVA today and discover your perfect urban retreat in NIBM.
